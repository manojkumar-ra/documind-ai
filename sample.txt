Johnny Depp is an American actor, producer, and musician born on June 9, 1963.
He is best known for his role as Captain Jack Sparrow in Pirates of the Caribbean.
Johnny Depp has won a Golden Globe Award and received three Academy Award nominations.
He starred in many Tim Burton films including Edward Scissorhands, Sleepy Hollow, and Dark Shadows.
Johnny Depp played Willy Wonka in Charlie and the Chocolate Factory in 2005.
He played the Mad Hatter in Alice in Wonderland in 2010.
Johnny Depp is also a musician and plays guitar in a band called Hollywood Vampires.
Hollywood Vampires band members include Alice Cooper and Joe Perry.
Johnny Depp won a highly publicized defamation lawsuit against Amber Heard in 2022.
He started his acting career in the 1984 horror film A Nightmare on Elm Street.
Johnny Depp is also known for playing Barnabas Collins in Dark Shadows.
